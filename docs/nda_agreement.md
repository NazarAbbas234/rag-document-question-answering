# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

## Clause 1: Definition of Confidential Information
Confidential Information includes all proprietary data, trade secrets, and business strategies disclosed.

## Clause 2: Obligations
The Receiving Party shall not disclose Confidential Information to any third party.

## Clause 3: Exclusions
Information that is publicly available or independently developed shall not be considered Confidential.

## Clause 4: Term
The obligations of confidentiality shall last for 2 years from the Effective Date.
