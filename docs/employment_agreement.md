# Employment Agreement

## Clause 1: Position
The Employee is hired as a Software Engineer and will report directly to the Manager.

## Clause 2: Compensation
The Employee shall receive a monthly salary of $5,000, payable on the last business day of each month.

## Clause 3: Confidentiality
The Employee shall not disclose any trade secrets or confidential information during or after employment.

## Clause 4: Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
